FAQ

How often does the catalog change?

Semi-frequently — new products are added and old ones removed as trends shift, not on a fixed schedule.

Is this a single-product store?

No. HotsTuff is intentionally multi-product and rotates its lineup based on what's currently trending and selling.

How fast does a new trend show up?

We watch trend and sell-through data daily, so a rising product can go live in hours — and once demand cools, it's retired to make room for what's next.

What if I want something that's already sold out or rotated off?

Trending items move fast by design, so availability isn't guaranteed to last. Every listing is vetted before it goes live, and if a product you bought rotates out of the catalog, your order, warranty, and support are unaffected.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed in 1–3 business days and typically arrive within 1–3 weeks, since items ship directly from our suppliers. You'll receive tracking by email when it's available. See Shipping & returns for full details.

What's your return policy?

Damaged, defective, wrong, or undelivered items get a free replacement or full refund within 30 days of delivery. Change-of-mind returns are accepted within 14 days (return shipping may apply). Reach our support team to start a return.
